Diabetic Disease Diagnosis

Understanding diabetes, symptoms, risks, and how to manage it effectively.

Details of Diabetes

Understanding Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic metabolic disorder where the body does not produce enough insulin or cannot use it effectively, leading to high blood sugar levels.

Types of Diabetes
  • Type 1: Autoimmune condition requiring insulin therapy.
  • Type 2: Linked to obesity, inactivity, and insulin resistance.
  • Gestational: Occurs during pregnancy and may increase Type 2 risk.
Common Symptoms
  • Excessive thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow wound healing
Potential Complications
  • Heart disease
  • Kidney damage
  • Nerve issues
  • Vision loss
Managing Diabetes
  • Healthy diet
  • Regular exercise
  • Blood sugar monitoring
  • Proper medication
Early Detection Matters

Early diagnosis and preventive measures can reduce complications and improve overall well-being.
