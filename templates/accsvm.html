What is a Genetic Algorithm ?

Genetic Algorithm (GA) is a powerful supervised machine learning algorithm used for both classification and regression tasks. Inspired by the process of natural selection, GA optimizes solutions by evolving a population of potential solutions through selection, crossover, and mutation.

How Does a Genetic Algorithm Work?

Random Forest Model works by following these steps:

  1. Initializes a population of potential solutions.
  2. Evaluates fitness of each individual.
  3. Uses selection, crossover, and mutation to evolve better solutions.
  4. Converges towards an optimal solution over generations.
