Prediabetes is a warning sign that you need to take action. By making small changes in your lifestyle, you can reduce your risk of developing type 2 diabetes.

üí° Healthy Lifestyle Choices

Managing prediabetes is easier than you think. Here are some key lifestyle changes:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fiber and low in processed sugar.
  • Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily.
  • Regularly monitor your blood sugar levels.
  • Get 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.

If you notice any of the following symptoms, consult a doctor:

  • Slow-healing wounds or frequent infections.
  • Blurry vision and sudden weight changes.
